Eritrea extends to about 117 thousand square kilometers in northeastern Africa on the Red Sea coast. It borders with Sudan in the northwest, Ethiopia in the south, and Djibouti in the southeast. Population is about 5 million inhabitants, who belongs to different ethnic groups and cultures. The largest and capital city -Asmara- is situated at an altitude of 2325 meters on the northwest edge of Valley of big rocks.
